Our Story

Azer's bakey is headquartered in Paterson, New Jersey. Our Company was founded in 2025. Since then, we have continuously grown our sales and added new product lines to our offerings. While we have experience in the overall food bakery and distribution industry, we focus primarily on baking traditional premium "handmade" breads, pastries, and desserts.

Due to the fact that we are a family owned and operated business, we are constantly driven to produce unique and superior products that satisfy the need of our customers from all around the world. Our team is passionate about our high standards and we use top quality wholesome ingredients and all our products are baked in brick hearth ovens and Kosher + Halal certified.

In addition to our deliveries to supermarkets, restaurants, other bakeries, hotels, and special events, for your convenient we also deliver our products via US cariers.
