Our Bread is Always Fresh

Straight from our oven — never processed

Our bread is baked fresh daily—warm, soft, and full of natural moisture. This freshness comes from the steam inside and the absence of preservatives. Left unwrapped, bread begins to stale within a day or two as moisture escapes. That's why we recommend enjoying our loaves shortly after baking for the best taste and texture.
